Timeline
  • JUN 1, 1937- Morgan Freeman was born in 1937. He was born in Memphis Tennessee. His parents were Mayme Edna and Morgan Freeman.

  • MAY 11, 1949-Freeman took his talents to the radio. He performed skits over the air. He did this in Nashville Tennesse.
  • SEP 7, 1949-Morgan turns 12. He won a state wide drama competiton. He did it all while still attending school.
  • JUN 9, 1955-In 1955, he graduated from Broad Street. He turned down a partial drama scholarship from Jackson State University, Instead to work as a mechanic in the United States Air Force.
  • FEB 15, 1961-Morgan turned 24. He moved to Los Angeles. He worked as a transcript clerk at Los Angeles Community College.
